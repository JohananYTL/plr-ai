WEDDING STYLES

Located on a private island off the West Coast of Malaysia in the Straits of Malacca, Pangkor Laut Resort is as unique as the natural beauty that surrounds it. Here, sophisticated romance plays against the breathtaking views of a tropical paradise offering the perfect backdrop to bring your family and friends together in celebration of your union.




  • PANGKOR LAUT BEACH WEDDING
  • WEDDING AT SEA
  • LANTERN COURTYARD WEDDING
  • THE ESTATES WEDDING


Exchange vows and leave the first footprints together at the award-winning Emerald Bay as the sun sets. The picturesque setting weaves in romantic elements of a shell-lined petal strewn aisle, rustic driftwood arch and fragrant white flowers. Toast in celebration over a five-course seafood dinner on the private beach as waves gently lap at the shore.

    This signature experience includes:

  • • Couples Spa Experience at Spa Village Pangkor Laut (recommended pre-ceremony)
    A Malay wedding ceremony is renowned for its pomp and pageantry, practised for hundreds of years through generations. The custom of preparing the bride and bridegroom is a highly treasured family secret in the Malay culture. The Secrets of Puteri Lindungan Bulan (for her) and Panglima Bukit Gantang (for him) are royal spa treatments befitting a warrior and his princess based on ancient Malay traditions comprising of a traditional Malay massage, hair treatment ritual, herbal scrub, fragrant bath, body steam bath and herbal tonic drink
  • • Bridal bouquet and boutonniere for groom
  • • Shell-lined petal strewn aisle and rustic gazebo with trailing flowers
  • • Christian pastor or wedding celebrant blessing
  • • Celebratory cocktails with canapés
  • • Five-course seafood dinner with a bottle of champagne
  • • Two-tier wedding cake
    select from butter cake, chocolate fudge, fruitcake or cheesecake flavours

  • • Complimentary two nights honeymoon stay inclusive of accommodation and breakfast at any of the properties in the YTL Hotels’ luxury collection in Malaysia or Bali with every wedding experience booked (valid for 18 months from the wedding date)






Set sail aboard the YTL Lady I, a 65ft motor yacht, and anchor at the magnificent Emerald Bay for an elegant, intimate ceremony at sea. Exchange vows on the petal strewn deck as the sun sets before cocktails and canapés are served as the resident band serenades. Cruise back to the island where a sumptuous seafood banquet and champagne toast on the beach awaits.
