GETTING HERE

AIR TRAVEL

Travel to Pangkor Laut from either Kuala Lumpur International Airport (KLIA), Kuala Lumpur International Airport 2 (klia2), Sultan Abdul Aziz Shah Airport (SZB) or Sultan Azlan Shah Airport (IPH). Travellers from Singapore can connect to Sultan Azlan Shah Airport directly via these airlines:

LOCATION AIRLINES
Singapore Firefly , Tigerair and Malindo Air

The journey from the airport continues with a car transfer to Pangkor Laut Resort's office at Marina Island Pangkor, followed by a speedboat transfer to Pangkor Laut Resort.


Helicopter transfers direct to the resort are available, do enquire with travelcentre@ytlhotels.com .



LAND TRANSFER


Private vehicle transfer between the airport and the resort’s office at Marina Island Pangkor can be arranged at a fee.

Check-in to the resort is conducted at the resort’s office at Marina Island Pangkor before you are escorted to the jetty for a speedboat transfer to Pangkor Laut Resort.

At the resort’s office, mineral water will be provided during check-in. Wireless internet access is provided in the lounge.

Should you wish to drive, a paid carpark is available at Marina Island Pangkor's car park, with a walking distance of approximately 10 minutes to the resort’s office.
