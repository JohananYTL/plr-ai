THE STRAITS TO LUXURY EXPERIENCE

Representing the finest in luxury hospitality, discover The Straits to Luxury Experience, an exclusive 5 day, 4 night charter that departs from Pangkor Laut in the late evening for a romantic cruise to Phuket with island stops along the way. Available only from December until May, our cruise experience is perfect for an intimate group of 6 persons or less.

Set sail onboard YTL Lady II, our 85 footer Princess motor yacht known for its power, agility and elegance, it is suited to long-range cruising with a secluded foredeck seating area. Dedicated marine professionals and a private hostess/chef will cater to your every whim. Your cruise experience is encapsulated below.

For further enquiries or reservations, please contact travelcentre@ytlhotels.com.my or +60 3 2783 1000.

PANGKOR LAUT – LANGKAWI – KO ROK NOK

Arrive at Langkawi for breakfast while your Captain disembarks for immigration clearance. Explore exotic mangroves, a bat cave, limestone rock formations, and local fishing villages or embark on a spot of eagle watching before enjoying a delightful seafood themed lunch. Choose to enjoy a leisurely cycling tour around villages or discover legends and attractions on an island tour. Have a coffee by the beach before departing for Ko Rok Nok in the evening. Retire to your luxurious cabin after an indulgent dinner.


KO ROK NOK – KO KAI

Ko Rok Nok is an uninhabited island off the west coast of Thailand. With clear blue waters, a white-sand beach and a beautiful coral garden; choose to swim, explore or simply laze in the sun as your personal chef prepares a picnic lunch on the beach. As the sun sets, a barbecue dinner on the beach with a selection of grilled meats and seafood awaits. Dine at leisure and have a peaceful slumber before departing in the morning to your next island paradise.

Awake refreshed for breakfast and be the first to spot the strange rock formation at the tip of Ko Kai island. Nicknamed as Chicken Island, the rock formation resembles the head and neck of a chicken. Steep cliffs form one end of the island while the other end offers beautiful beaches along a peninsula. Snorkel in the warm, clear waters and explore the different coral reef teeming with marine life. Enjoy lunch on board amidst the islets surrounding this beautiful island before heading to Ko Phi Phi for your next stop.


KO PHI PHI – PHUKET

Ko Phi Phi comprises of 6 islands and forms part of Hadnopparattara-Ko Phi Phi National Park, home to an abundance of corals and marine life. The islands came to worldwide prominence when the second largest island was featured in The Beach, a British-American film in 2000. Take in the breath-taking scenery of limestone mountains and long white sandy beaches against the brilliant hues of the setting sun before settling down for another memorable dinner on board.

Continue on your journey in the morning and arrive at Phuket, berthing at Ao Po Grand Marina. Consider an extended stay at The Surin Phuket or your preferred choice of accommodation while in Phuket. Should you wish us to arrange your departure, there is also a return journey available on our Princess yacht.
