SEASONAL OFFERS

Dial “0” for bookings and enquiries.



AROS KRISTANG

Indulge in a hearty one-plate feast with the Kristang specialties of the day, come with a portion of rice, meat, vegetables and is served with a glass of refreshing iced tea.

Available on weekdays | 12pm to 3pm

RM35 nett

MAJESTIC HOUR AT THE BAR

Positioned by the lobby, the bar is the perfect place to unwind and relax, warmly welcoming guests for a round of drinks and casual chats.Take 50% off your favourite house pours during Majestic Hour at The Bar!*

Available on weekdays | 5pm to 7pm

*Only applicable for selected beers, liqour and glasses of house wines

KRISTANG NIGHT

Celebrate Kristang culture with all the classics! Happening monthly, this night of heritage, presenting lively folk dance and music, an artisan market of local crafts and authentic Kristang cuisine.

Available on 11 April and 21 May | 7pm – 10:30pm

RM150 nett per adult and RM75 nett per child (aged 3 - 11)
